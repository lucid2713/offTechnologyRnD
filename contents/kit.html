ENO KIT is a DIY(Do It Yourself) kit for e-waste analog synthesizer.


ENO KIT helps you build your own e-waste synth easily and amusedly.


ENO KIT is designed as a part of 'off technology R&D'.


ENO KIT is made by MINA (VITAMINA).
